About

Xeni/a Kitzoglou grew up in Athens, Greece and graduated from the Audiovisual Arts Department at Ionian University in 2024, where she also completed the Pedagogical Sufficiency Program. She has coordinated Art Therapy workshops at festivals, in Greek prisons, and within academic programs in Malta. Her main mediums include performance, video art, and photography.
